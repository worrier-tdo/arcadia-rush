FAQ

Answers to common questions about Arcadia Rush and Desert Dash.

Is it mobile-friendly?

Yes. Tap to jump and responsive canvas scaling make it easy on phones.

How are scores saved?

High score and leaderboard entries are saved in your browser via localStorage.

Can I reset the leaderboard?

In your browser console, run localStorage.removeItem('ar_leaderboard').

What keys are supported?

Space/Up to jump. P to pause. R to save & restart.

Can I change colors or text?

Yes—edit CSS variables in assets/css/style.css and copy in HTML.
